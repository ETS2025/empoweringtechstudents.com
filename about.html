About Us

We empower tech students who have little to no access to necessary tech devices required to enhance their learning experience, providing them with free, reliable and necessary tech devices.

Our Mission

At Empowering Tech Students, our mission is to bridge the gap between tech students and the devices they need to succeed, empowering them to achieve their full potential. We believe that every student deserves equal access to the technology that will help them succeed academically and professionally.

Image Description of Our Mission

Our Problem Statement

The world is becoming increasingly digital, and the demand for tech-savvy professionals is on the rise. However, many students face a significant barrier to entry: a lack of access to devices. This can hinder their ability to complete coursework, participate in online learning, and gain the skills they need to succeed in the tech industry.

Image Description of Our Problem Statement

Our Solution

Empowering Tech Student, is dedicated to providing affordable devices to tech students in need. We work with device manufacturers, educational institutions, and other partners to source high-quality devices at discounted prices. Our goal is to make it possible for every tech student to have access to the devices they need to succeed.

Image Description of Our Solution

Our Values

  • Accessibility: We believe that every student deserves access to the technology they need to succeed.
  • Reliability: We are committed to providing tech students with their most trusted tech devices.
  • Quality: We only provide high-quality devices that meet the needs of tech students.
  • Community: We believe in building a community of tech students, educators, and industry professionals who share our mission and values.
