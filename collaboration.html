Our Mission

We understand the challenges many tech students face, including limited access to resources like laptops. That's why we're committed to providing support and opportunities for students to pursue their passion for tech, regardless of their financial situation.

My Journey in Tech

By Paul Ejime Mormah, Information Technology Student, National Open University of Nigeria

As an Information Technology student, I've experienced firsthand the impact of supportive partnerships. My father, who is now in the bossom of the Lord, strongly encouraged my education. Meanwhile, my uncle, who sponsored my academics, advised me to pursue IT.

Everything changed during my 24-week internship at an IT company. That experience opened my eyes to the vast possibilities and opportunities in the tech world. I saw firsthand how technology was transforming businesses, industries, and lives. My mindset shifted, and I began to see myself as a tech giant, a pioneer in my own right.

Despite facing challenges, such as not having a personal laptop due to financial constraints, I have found ways to stay engaged in my field. With access to a smartphone and occasional use of borrowed laptops, I've been able to explore my passion for tech and web design.

Today, I am hungry to explore the depths of the tech world. I am driven by a desire to learn, innovate, and make a meaningful impact. As a web design enthusiast, I'm excited to combine my passion for tech with my love for writing.
